Instructions

In this word guessing game, you can guess the color of a randomly displayed word. Here's how you can play:


A word will be displayed on the screen. Your objective is to correctly guess the color of the word corresponding to its value.

When you click on a color button, the displayed word will change to that color.

If you guess the correct color, your score will increase by 1. If you guess wrong, your score will decrease by 1.

Start the game by clicking the "Start" button. The game ends when the time runs out.

When the game ends, the highest score is updated, if necessary.

You can reset the game at any time by clicking the "Reset" button. This will reset your score, time, and the game state.


Enjoy playing and have fun trying to guess the color of the word!
